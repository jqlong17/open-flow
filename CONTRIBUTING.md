# Contributing to Open Flow

Thanks for contributing to Open Flow.

This project is open source under the [MIT License](LICENSE). At the same time, the project may also be distributed through paid channels, including but not limited to the Mac App Store.

## Before You Submit a PR

By opening a pull request to this repository, you confirm that:

1. You wrote the contribution yourself, or you otherwise have the legal right to submit it.
2. You are allowed to contribute the code, documentation, assets, or other materials under the repository's open-source license.
3. You agree that your contribution may be used, modified, sublicensed, and distributed by the project maintainers in both:
   - open-source distributions
   - commercial or paid distributions, including app-store distribution
4. You understand that your contribution will become part of the Open Flow project and may be maintained, relicensed in combination with other material where permitted, packaged, and sold as part of project distributions.

If you are not comfortable with that, please do not submit the contribution.

## Contribution Scope

Good contribution targets include:

- bug fixes
- documentation improvements
- tests
- platform compatibility fixes
- focused UX improvements
- performance and reliability improvements

Please avoid very large, cross-cutting changes without discussion first.

## Pull Request Notes

When opening a PR, please include:

- what changed
- why it changed
- how you tested it
- any user-facing impact

If your change affects packaging, distribution, permissions, pricing-related flows, or version differences between the GitHub edition and the App Store edition, call that out explicitly in the PR description.

## Questions

If you want to contribute but are unsure whether something fits the project direction, open an issue first.
