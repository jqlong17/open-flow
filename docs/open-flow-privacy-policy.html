Open Flow Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Open Flow is a macOS app for local offline voice input. This privacy policy explains what the Mac App Store version of Open Flow does with your data and permissions.

1. Core privacy approach

The Mac App Store version of Open Flow is designed to perform its core speech-to-text workflow locally on your Mac. The app does not require an account and does not use the app for advertising or user tracking.

2. Data collection

Open Flow does not collect personal data from you for analytics, advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties in the Mac App Store version.

If you choose to contact support by email, you may voluntarily provide information such as your email address and the details included in your message.

3. Permissions used by the app

  • Microphone: used to capture your voice for local speech-to-text input.
  • Accessibility: used to insert transcribed text into the active text field.
  • Input Monitoring: used to detect the user-configured global hotkey.

4. Audio and transcription

Audio used for the core voice input workflow is processed locally on device. The app’s main Mac App Store workflow is designed around offline speech recognition.

5. Network use

The Mac App Store version is designed for a local-first workflow. If the app makes any secure network connection, it uses standard HTTPS/TLS protections. The Mac App Store version does not use network access for advertising or behavioral tracking.

6. Data sharing

Open Flow does not sell your personal data and does not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

7. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same URL.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: jqlong@me.com

Open Flow 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 4 月 9 日

Open Flow 是一款用于 macOS 的本地离线语音输入应用。本隐私政策说明 Mac App Store 版本的 Open Flow 如何处理数据以及为什么需要相关系统权限。

1. 核心隐私原则

Mac App Store 版本的 Open Flow 以本地离线语音转文字为核心路径。应用不要求创建账号， 也不将应用用于广告投放或用户跟踪。

2. 数据收集

Mac App Store 版本的 Open Flow 不会为了分析、广告、画像或向第三方出售而收集您的个人数据。

如果您主动通过电子邮件联系支持，我们可能会收到您自愿提供的信息，例如电子邮箱地址和邮件正文内容。

3. 应用使用的权限

  • 麦克风：用于采集您的语音并在本机进行语音转文字。
  • 辅助功能：用于将转写后的文本输入到当前活动文本框。
  • 输入监控：用于监听您配置的全局快捷键。

4. 音频与转写

用于核心语音输入流程的音频会在本机处理。当前 Mac App Store 版本以离线识别为主要工作方式。

5. 网络使用

Mac App Store 版本以本地优先为设计原则。如果应用发生安全网络连接，也仅使用标准 HTTPS/TLS 保护方式。Mac App Store 版本不会将网络能力用于广告或行为跟踪。

6. 数据共享

Open Flow 不会出售您的个人数据，也不会为了广告目的向第三方共享您的个人数据。

7. 政策更新

如果本隐私政策发生变化，更新后的版本会发布在同一链接地址。

8. 联系方式
